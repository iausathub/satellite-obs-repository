About SCORE

The Satellite Constellation Observation Repository (SCORE) is a project developed and maintained by the International Astronomical Union Centre for the Protection of the Dark and Quiet Sky from Satellite Constellation Interference (IAU CPS).

SCORE's data includes reference values from SatChecker for the time and location of each observation - SatChecker makes use of data obtained from Space-Track and Celestrak to predict satellite positions. For more infomation on that data, please visit the SatChecker documentation.

All data in SCORE is made available under the SCORE Use Policy, and should be cited with the following statements:

"This research made use of data and/or services provided by the International Astronomical Union Centre for the Protection of the Dark and Quiet Sky from Satellite Constellation Interference (IAU CPS) SatHub. The development of SCORE has been supported by the National Science Foundation (NSF) under grant number: AST 2332736. SCORE is hosted at the NSF NOIRLab."
